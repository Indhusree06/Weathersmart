### Step 1: Add Items to Your Wardrobe
Before you can see meaningful analytics, you need items in your wardrobe:
1. Go to **Wardrobes** page
2. Click **Add Item**
3. Upload photos and add details for your clothing items
4. Tag items with seasons, occasions, and weather conditions

### Step 2: Track Your Wears
The more you track, the better your insights:
1. When you wear an item, update its wear count
2. Mark when you last wore it
3. This data powers the "Most Worn" and "Least Worn" analytics

### Step 3: Save Outfits
Create outfit combinations:
1. Use the **AI Outfit Picker** to get recommendations
2. Save your favorite combinations
3. Mark special occasions
4. These appear in your Analytics dashboard

### Step 4: Access Analytics
Navigate to the Analytics page from any page:
- Click **Analytics** in the navigation menu
- Or visit `/analytics` directly

## ğŸ“ Pro Tips

### Maximize Your Wardrobe Health Score

1. **Category Balance (+20 points)**
   - Have at least one item in each category
   - Tops, Bottoms, Shoes, Outerwear

2. **Wear Distribution (+20 points)**
   - Wear all your items regularly
   - Don't let items sit unworn

3. **Item Condition (+10 points)**
   - Keep items in good condition
   - Mark condition accurately

### Get Better AI Recommendations

1. **Rate Consistently**: Rate at least 5-10 outfits
2. **Be Honest**: Your ratings train the AI
3. **Explain Preferences**: Add notes to items about what you like

### Build a Versatile Wardrobe

Use analytics to identify:
- **Missing Categories**: What don't you have?
- **Color Gaps**: Neutrals? Accent colors?
- **Season Gaps**: Ready for all weather?

## â“ FAQ

**Q: Why is my wardrobe health low?**  
A: Check the three factors: category balance, wear distribution, item condition. Focus on the weakest area.

**Q: I don't have any AI outfits to rate.**  
A: Visit the **AI Outfit Picker** page to generate personalized recommendations first.

**Q: My least worn items are formal wear - should I donate them?**  
A: Not necessarily! Items for special occasions will naturally have lower wear counts. Consider the item's purpose.

**Q: How often should I check analytics?**  
A: Weekly or monthly is good. Use it before shopping or during seasonal wardrobe switches.

**Q: Can I export my analytics?**  
A: Not yet, but this feature is planned for a future update.
